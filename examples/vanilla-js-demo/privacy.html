Welcome to Webgazer Privacy Demo

Before we begin, we want to inform you about how your data is handled:

  • Local Processing: All eye tracking and video analysis happens entirely within your browser.
  • No Image Uploads: Your webcam video stream is never sent to any server.
  • Voluntary: You can stop tracking at any time, which immediately kills the webcam process.
  • Open Source: This library is fully open source and transparent about its data handling.

By clicking "I Consent", you allow this page to access your webcam locally for eye tracking purposes.
