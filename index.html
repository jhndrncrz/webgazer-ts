👁️ Webgazer Minimal Example

A simple demonstration of eye tracking in the browser

How to Use:

  1. Click "Start Webgazer" and allow camera access
  2. Click "Start Calibration" and click on each calibration point
  3. Move your eyes around - the system will track your gaze!
  4. The red dot shows where the system thinks you're looking
Ready to start. Click "Start Webgazer" to begin.
